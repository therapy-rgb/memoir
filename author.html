Author

Methodology was written by Marcus Berley. Other publication experiments by the author include M, The Giraffe, MoMo, Therapy 101, The Intern Dispatch, and Suburban Dad Mode. His professional website is Marcus Berley Psychotherapy, LLC.

A handful of poems can be found published by in parentheses. Additional unpublished letters, poems and notebooks can be requested from the archives, currently located in Romariz, Portugal. Works not to be confused with those of Marc Berley.
